Airtable Gmail Extension Configuration Tutorial:

1) Go to Airtable.com and log into your account or sign up if you do not have an account.

2) Once in your account, you will see multiple tiles containing your bases; click on or create a new base to use.

3) Once in the desired base, click on the "Help" button in the upper right corner.

4) On the "Help " drop down menu, you will see a "Use with Gmail extension" option. Click this button to configure your extension to work with the current base/table you are viewing/have active.

5) If Gmail is already open during this configuration process, make sure to refresh the page.
